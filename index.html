a farmer's market right at your doorstep

Santi's Fruit Store is a family business that brings you the opportunity to get the finest fruits and vegetables right at your doorstep.
With the COVID-19 pandemic, farmer markets weren't able to ... so with our online store we want to ensure you can get the same quality as you did in a farmer's market or in a physical store.

choose between out finest seasonal products

We offer in our online store a selection of the best products according to each season and also offer a subscription box that will be shipped to you with our finest, seasonal greens.

fast shipping -- and ecological

Your order will be shipped in a period of 24-48 hours time by our electric, refrigerated truck fleet.

We also have a physical store, where you can visit us and have a chat!
